Lasagna


Description

Lasagna is an Italian dish made of stacked layers of wide, flat pasta sheets alternating with fillings such as ragu, bechamel sauce, vegetables, cheeses, and seasonings and spices.

Ingredients

  • Beef
  • Spaghetti sauce
  • Cheeses: cottage, mozzarella, and Parmesan
  • Eggs
  • Seasonings: dried parsley, salt, and black pepper
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Water

Steps

  1. Cook and drain the ground beef, then stir in the spaghetti sauce and simmer.
  2. Combine the cottage cheese, 2 cups of mozzarella, eggs, half of the Parmesan, and seasonings.
  3. Assemble the lasagna according to the detailed recipe.
  4. Bake, covered, for 45 minutes.
  5. Uncover and continue baking for 10 minutes.
